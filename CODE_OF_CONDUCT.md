# Code of Conduct

> *"War. War never changes. But how we treat each other in the wasteland? That part's up to us."*

Adapted from the [Contributor Covenant v3.0](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/3/0/code_of_conduct/).

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make this community a place where everyone can contribute freely — regardless of who you are, where you come from, what you look like, or which faction you side with. No gatekeeping. No hierarchy of "real" contributors. If you're here to build, you belong here.

## The Rules (Keep It Simple)

### Do

- Be kind and honest — even when you disagree
- Respect different viewpoints and experiences
- Give and receive feedback like an adult
- Own your mistakes and fix the damage
- Help others when you can — we're all wandering the same wasteland

### Don't

- Harass, insult, or attack people — in public or in private
- Use sexualized language or imagery — this isn't that kind of settlement
- Troll, derail, or stir up drama for sport
- Share other people's private information without consent
- Stereotype, discriminate, or punch down
- Spam, self-promote, or drop unsolicited ads — no one likes a wasteland merchant who won't shut up

## Enforcement — The Overseer Protocol

Nobody wants rules. But every community eventually meets someone who ruins it for everyone else. When that happens:

1. **Warning** — A private message explaining what went wrong. Most things end here.
2. **Cooldown** — Temporary restriction from community spaces. Go touch some irradiated grass.
3. **Suspension** — Longer break with conditions for return. Think of it as a trip to the Vault.
4. **Permanent Ban** — Reserved for serious or repeated violations. You've been exiled to the Glowing Sea. No return.

Enforcement decisions are made by the maintainers. We're not perfect, but we'll be fair and transparent about it.

## Scope

This applies everywhere this community operates — issues, PRs, discussions, and any space where you're representing this project. If you wouldn't say it in front of the whole settlement, don't say it here.
